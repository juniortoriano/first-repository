Description

Great White Sharks are a species of large mackerel shark which can be found in the coastal surface waters of all the major oceans

Weight

The weight of these animals are 1,905 kg (4,200 lb)

Size

The females are much larger at 6.1 m (20 ft) while the smaller males are 3.4 to 4.0 m (11 to 13 ft)

Color

The color of the sharks are normally grey with their underside being white
